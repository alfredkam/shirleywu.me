    My love for beauty in all its forms have inspired me to become successful in both luxury retail as well as becoming a professional classical pianist. Being fully conscious that presentation is the key as well as one needs to be 100% focused at practise and on the stage, I worked diligently to balance these two completely opposite philosophies in life. My ultimate goal is to present to the world the beauty of Western classical music, a true gift that transcends all culture and religion, a true beauty that all gods have created.

    Our story

    Started in a shared apartment with a single bed in Toronto Chinatown, I embarked on a journey to search for my cultural identity through art.


    Prelude

    The relationship between China and North America is deepening and the rapidly growing emphasis on the foreign marketplace is increasingly game-changing; Opus One Music is an investigation of the captivating possibilities, the infinite opportunities, and the happenstance of international and sociopolitical systems at the feet of economically motivated agendas.

    Our Mission

    Influence the world through the art medium.
    Bring communities together.
    Solve conflicts in business.

    Who we are

    We are trilingual, tricultrual, we are second generation Chinese American/Canadian. We are the bridge between the West and the East.

    What we do

    Organize classical chamber music parties in high end residences.
